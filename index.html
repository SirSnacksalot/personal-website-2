I was raised in Southern California sunshine and wildfires.

Growing up, I was really interested in the natural world, in connecting with my emotions through music, and in long distance running.

I had always wanted to be a scientist, and planned on being a physicist when I first started going to MIT.

But after some time there, I saw that spending my time figuring out the mysteries of the universe wasn't really for me.

Instead, I found excitement in storytelling and in computer science.

Today, as a recent graduate, I have a few big aspirations, like directing a feature film and helping people respect and love one another on a global scale.

I'm open to new friendships and experiences that will one day make for a riveting tale to tell my grandkids.
